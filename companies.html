Trading

Pathivara group is determined to cater the consumer demands through its trading network operating in most of the sub-metropolitan cities of Morang and Sunsari. We are authorized distributors and traders of varied range of consumer products which includes essential commodities as well as construction raw materials. Some of our trading areas are:
 Distributor of Salt Trading corporation.
 Distributor of Reckitt Benckiser products.
 Distributor of consumer good brands viz. Colgate, Druk, Cadbury, Bikaji, MDH.
 Distributor of Mahashakti soap and chemical industries.
 Distributor for hair dyeing products (Vasmol, Streax) of Hygienic Research Institute.
 Distributor of Tokla Tea, bestselling tea brand of Nepal Tea Development, Ltd.
 Distributor of Shivam Cement Ltd.  Distributor of Bitumen  Distributor of Maruti Trading, Volvo Heavy Equipment
